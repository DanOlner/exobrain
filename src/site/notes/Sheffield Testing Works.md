## [[https://www.gracesguide.co.uk/Sheffield_Testing_Works\|Grace's guide]]:  

1880 [Thomas Nash](https://www.gracesguide.co.uk/Thomas_Nash "Thomas Nash") established an independent testing and experimenting works in Sheffield. This produced accurate work, with the result that the business grew rapidly. He then added a chemical laboratory to the mechanical testing laboratory. 

1889 [William Cleland](https://www.gracesguide.co.uk/William_Cleland "William Cleland") appointed manager

1892 Nash handed control of the business to his son [Tom](https://www.gracesguide.co.uk/Thomas_Nash_\(d.1910\) "Thomas Nash (d.1910)")
