Schneider & Vipond. CESinfo working paper 2023.

“While several papers find that new jobs were generated to replace those lost to new labor-saving technologies, access to these new employment opportunities was stratified by gender, age, education, and geographical location. Incumbents whose skills were rendered obsolete by new technologies may have had limited opportunity to move into newly created jobs.” p.16
